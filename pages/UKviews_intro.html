Geography of UK

Before we proceed, let's introduce a little of UK's physical geaography. United Kingdom is actually an Island Country, which means that the major part of it's territory consists in a group of islands. Some smallers, some biggers, an they are spread through England, Wales, Scottland and Northern Irelan. For this reason, beaches and lakes are super common in it's nature, and some of it's beaches pass some time of the years underneath the ocean's water due to the tidal level.

The image beside this text is a Topography of UK's territory. As you can see, there's many green and yellow to red parts. These colors on it's topography means 2 things: Low terrain and Moutains. That's right, a pretty big part of UK is based on low terrain and moutains! They give UK outstanding views and unique places that are totally worth traveling in!

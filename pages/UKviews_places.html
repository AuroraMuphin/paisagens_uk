Some United Kingdom places!

Let's meet some of United Kingdom places that can show a bit of it's beauty!

Kynance Cove

Kynance Cove it's a beach located in England, where most part of it is underneath the water, and just get accessible at low-tide. It's considered one of the most beautiful beaches in the world!

Mt. Snowdown

This is the biggest moutain in Wales, with an outstading view! In a clear day, it's even possible to see Ireland, Scotland and England. Luckily, "The Snowdown Moutain Railway" brings tourists to it's top on late March to early November.

Swallow Falls

This waterfall is also located in Wales, which happens to be a series of cascades from the river Llugwy that's 3.7 KM long. You can watch it from the hotel "Swallow Falls Hotel", with a staircase that runs alongside it.

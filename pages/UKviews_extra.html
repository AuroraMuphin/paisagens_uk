Fauna and Flora of UK.

United Kingdom is a big Archipelago that extends through the north and south parts of Europe. That means, it's climate is mostly temperate, but it get colder near the north parts, so you can see sub-tropical to subarctic regions. It's also good to notice that the climate there have high levels of precipitation, which means that the clouds are havier almost all the time so the rain aesthetic is pretty present in the entire UK.

UK have a large amount of diversity in it's nature, that makes it a special places for animals. The high amount lakes in low terrains makes animals like otters, frogs and even axolotls in it's caves to be present in it's fauna. Also it's possible to see whales, orcas and even octopus in it's seas. But the most noticeable animal to be found in UK in it's forests are the Deers, that have many varieties like: Red Deer, Mutjac Deer, Fallow Deer, Roe Deer.
